Internet Cookies

Company’s Website utilizes internet cookies. Internet cookies are small data files that are deployed from a web server to your computing device. Internet cookies are used for different functions as described in this Section, including to allocate traffic to our Website between servers, to help combat fraud and malicious activities taken against our Website, to recall user preferences, and help store information contained in our online web forms.

Strictly Necessary Cookies – our Website and the Mini Site use strictly necessary cookies that enable the core functionality of our Website, including web traffic allocation, accessibility, and data security.

Advertising Cookies – Our Website and the Mini Site utilize advertising cookies deployed by third parties, which collect information from your web browser, including your web history and an internet identifier for purposes of displaying advertisements that could be of an interest to you.

Analytics Cookies – Our Website and the Mini Site utilize analytics cookies that are deployed by third parties to collect information about your interaction with our Website or the Mini Site, as applicable, including information about your computing device, internet browser, internet service provider domain, length of time on our Website or the Mini Site, which are used for purposes of understanding how our users browse our ecommerce web pages.

  • We use Strictly Necessary Cookies to enable you to use the applicable Website or Mini Site. We also use the information derived from Strictly Necessary Cookies to combat fraud and malicious activity on our Website and in connection with processing any transaction with us.
  • We use Advertising Cookies for purposes of placing targeted advertisements directed at you concerning our products.
  • We use Analytics Cookies to better understand how our users interact with the Website or Mini Site and to optimize your user experience by tailoring each web page to the most popular and commonly used computing devices and web browsers.

Your Options for Internet Cookies

Depending upon the internet web browser that you utilize to browse our Website, you may have the option to block or reject internet cookies. For more information on your options for blocking or rejecting cookies, please consult your internet web browser’s help file. Please note that if you block or reject internet cookies, some functionality on the Website will no longer work properly.

California Consumer Data Rights Under the CCPA

Under the CCPA, a California consumer is afforded certain data rights, which include the right to request information from a business that collects personal information about the consumer, including:

  • The categories of personal information, as that term is defined under the CCPA, that the business has collected from that consumer;
  • The categories of sources from which the personal information is collected;
  • The business or commercial purpose for collecting, selling, or sharing personal information;
  • The categories of third parties with whom the business shares personal information; and
  • The specific pieces of personal information that the business has collected about that consumer.

You may submit a request for the foregoing information to us via telephone at (888) 900-3933 or via email at answers@studioshed.com.

Right to Request to Know Personal Information

Pursuant to the CCPA, a California consumer has the right to know what personal information the business has collected about the consumer. You may exercise your right to know by submitting a request to us via telephone at (888) 900-3933 or via email at answers@studioshed.com. Within ten (10) business days of receipt of your request, we will provide you with an email confirmation that we have received your request.

When you submit a request to know, we will collect your first name, last name, and email address. If the information that you submit matches the information that we have on file about you, we will notify you when your information is ready for you to access. We will provide the specific pieces of personal information obtained from the consumer in a format that is easily understandable to the average consumer, and to the extent technically feasible, in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format to enable to consumer to forward this data on to another entity.

Right to Request Deletion of Personal Information

The CCPA affords California consumers the right to request the deletion of the personal information that a business maintains about the consumer, subject to the specific exceptions detailed in Cal Civ. Code § 1798.105. If you would like to exercise your right to deletion, you may do so via telephone at (888) 900-3933 or via email at answers@studioshed.com. Within ten (10) business days of receiving your request for deletion, we will provide you with an email to confirm receipt of your request.

Right to Rectification

In advance of the effective date of the California Privacy Rights Act of 2020 (“CPRA”), which becomes effective January 1, 2023, California consumers are afforded the right to request a business that maintains inaccurate personal information about the consumer correct such inaccurate personal information taking into account the nature of the personal information and the purposes of the processing of the personal information. If you would like to exercise your right to deletion, you may do so via telephone at (888) 900-3933 or via email at answers@studioshed.com. Within ten (10) business days of receiving your request for deletion, we will provide you with an email to confirm receipt of your request.

Responses to Requests to Know, Request for Deletion, and Requests for Rectification

We will respond to your requests to know, requests for deletion, or requests for rectification with the applicable information within forty-five (45) calendar days of your request; provided, however, that if we cannot verify your identity, we may contact you to extend the period of our response for an additional forty-five (45) calendar days.
