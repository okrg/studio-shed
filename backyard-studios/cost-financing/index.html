HOW MUCH WILL MY BACKYARD STUDIO COST?

DESIGN & PRICE

As you plan to build your backyard studio, you’ll likely find yourself asking some, or all, of the following questions:

  • • How much will my studio cost?
  • • Does the cost include shipping and installation?
  • • What if I need help financing my Studio Shed?
  • • What are the long-term values of building a studio?

The good news, when it comes to the cost and financing of your backyard studio, is that Studio Shed can help you along the way to get these questions answered. Whether it’s configuring the perfect studio at the right price point or assessing financing options, our goal is to give you the confidence to make your dreams a reality.
The costs of our backyard studios will vary depending on your specific design. Because Studio Sheds are fully customizable, you can choose to include or exclude certain elements like extra windows and doors. As you move through our 3D Design Center, the costs will be clearly displayed at all stages of the process so you know exactly what your studio will cost.

OTHER COSTS TO CONSIDER WHEN BUILDING YOUR BACKYARD STUDIO:

Foundation

The Signature Series is built to stand on a poured concrete or self-supported joisted floor system. Or consider one of our Quick-Ship models that can be installed on a turnkey foundation

Shipping

Cost of shipping will be calculated and added in our 3D Design Center before purchase. We ship to all 50 states.

Assembly

Our backyard sheds are prefabricated, flatpacked kit shipping right to your door. You can DIY the installation or use one of our trusted partners.
