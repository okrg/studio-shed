Construction
2x6 Framing and Zip System weatherproof wall panels.
Roof
Engineered rafter beams, 1/2” Zip System weatherproof roof panels, 26-gauge corrugated Galvalume roofing with exposed fasteners, James Hardie vented soffit.
Windows
Gable-style CloudLite double-pane, high-efficient windows. Fixed transom and vertical options. Operable, awning windows with screens.
Green Materials
FSC certified lumber, mixed-source recycled metal and materials throughout, optimized prefabricated production. Meets building codes throughout the US.
Siding
HardiePanel fiber cement siding in multiple colors. Galvalume metal and cedar wood options available.
Door
36” Therma-Tru full-lite fiberglass door, or 72” Therma-Tru full-lite fiberglass French door.
Hardware
Brushed aluminum trim and door/window hardware. Standard foundation attachment hardware included. (8) corner straps included.
Warranty
1 year manufacturer and workmanship warranty on all models. After 1 year, vendor specific warranties apply.

Ordering

Ordering a Studio Shed Portland Series model isn’t only easy, it’s fun. Many of our sizes are available for you to build, customize, and order online with our 3D Design Center.

1 Design

Start crafting your design in our 3D Design Center.

2 Site Plan / Permitting

Check with your local building authorities.

3 Order

Once your design is complete place your order.

4 Ship

All Studio Sheds arrive as a flat-packed panelized kit.

5 Install

Are you going to hire a contractor or DIY?

6 Enjoy!

You've done it! Kick back and enjoy the comfort of your backyard studio.

Installation

Our network of 3rd party build partners are experienced contractors and experts in the installation of our studios. Installation ranges from one week to several depending on the scope of the project. Ask about our complimentary, installer site consult.

DIY Install

This is a great option for the handy homeowner who enjoys a weekend project and wants to save money. Every DIY Studio Shed purchase includes the same installer training tools that we use with our own Certified Install Teams. Homeowner also could hire a trusted contractor to install and count on the same resources and training provided to our Professional Install teams.

Professional Install

Our network of 3rd party builders take delivery of your Studio Shed and install the Shell building kit of walls, roof and siding. Windows are pre-installed into the panels. Lifestyle interior also may be purchased for install into the Shell in the field. Installers also typically quote site work and other adds like step/deck.
