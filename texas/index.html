Have Questions?

We’re here to help! View our most frequently asked questions below or request a free consultation to get connected with a dedicated Studio Shed advisor.

Do you ship to Texas?

Yes, Studio Shed ships to all 50 U.S. States and select locations in Canada. You can get a shipping quote by building your Studio Shed in our 3D Design Center and entering your zip code when prompted.

How much will my Studio Shed cost?

Each Studio Shed costs a little different depending on the model, size, and customizations you select, as well as your installation type. Head over to our 3D Design Center to build your own Studio Shed and see a real-time quote for your design!

Do you offer financing?

Yes, financing is available through our 3rd party lending partners at Acorn Finance. Visit our financing page to learn more, check your offers, and see your estimated monthly rate without impacting your credit score.
